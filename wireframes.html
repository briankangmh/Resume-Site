Wireframes for an Imaginary Food Delivery App

For this assignment I was supposed to create wireframes for an imaginary app, with emphasis on typographic design as an integral part of the interface. I payed special attention to the proportion and color of all interface elements, including the text, to make the information architecture as clear and intuitive as possible. I created the wireframes using sketch
